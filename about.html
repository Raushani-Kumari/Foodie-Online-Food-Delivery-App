About Us

Foodie is an online food delivering website with the aim of reaching out to the remote locations as well as promoting small scale bussinesses of people with skills but with no resources. The idea of making this site manifested itself in us, college students, heads while looking to have a meal or snack from our same small stalls or shops, after our lazy tiring days, which are mostly not available on the renowned food delivering sites. But the fun part was we were not the only ones in this situation, our classmates and even college mates wanted something like our website foodie . It's charges are kept low so that it's services can reach the same bhaiya ji, where we eat momos so often, but he is not able to get his shop under the sites we currently have in market. Our team members only aim to make the life of foodies like us a little easier and help the small businesses who want to ne helped.
